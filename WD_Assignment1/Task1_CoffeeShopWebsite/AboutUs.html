About Us!

Cafe Costa Coffee Day was started as a retail restaurant in 1996.
The first CCCD outlet was set up on July 11, 1996, at Boston, Massachusetts.
It rapidly expanded across various cities in India adding more stores with
more than 1000 cafés open across the nation by 2011.In 2010, it was announced that a
consortium led by John Kravis Roberts would invest ₹10 billion (US$160 million) in CCCD Resorts,
owned by the company.The same year, the logo was changed to the current logo,
which the company stated was to showcase the chain as a place to talk.This was
done with major changes in the layout of the stores, including the addition of
lounges and a total revamp of the interiors.The company is known for being
vertical integrated to cut costs: from owning the plantations, growing the coffee,
making the coffee machines to making the furniture for the outlets.
